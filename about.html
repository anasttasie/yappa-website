yappa

what it is

yappa is a voice journal you can take anywhere. tap yap to record while you walk, drive, or pause — and we turn your words into a recap with tags, mood, and energy.

over time, yappa shows patterns in how you feel, so you can notice what helps and do more of it.

who it's for

yappa is for anyone who wants a clearer view of their inner world — people who feel overwhelmed, anxious, or just want a gentler way to spot patterns in their mood and energy.

whether you're busy, prefer talking to typing, or simply want a calm place to think out loud, yappa meets you where you are.

privacy note

we're privacy-first: your audio is deleted as soon as your entry is captured, and your journal stays yours with end-to-end encryption.
