about yappa

yappa is a voice journal you can use anywhere. tap yap to record while you walk, and we'll turn your words into a clean recap with tags, mood and energy.

it's for anyone who wants to understand their inner world better — people who feel overwhelmed, anxious, or just want a gentler way to notice patterns in how they feel over time.

we're privacy-first: your audio is deleted as soon as your entry is captured, and your journal stays yours.
